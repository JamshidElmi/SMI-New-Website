Our History

2009

Buffalo CarShare Founded

Started as Buffalo CarShare, bringing carsharing to Western New York with a focus on serving disadvantaged communities.

2012

Reddy Bikeshare Launch

Launched Reddy Bikeshare, expanding into shared micromobility and providing equitable transportation options across Buffalo.

2015

CDPHP Cycle! Partnership

Partnered with CDPHP to operate the CDPHP Cycle! bikeshare program in New York's Capital Region, scaling operations statewide.

2018

Shared Mobility Inc.

Rebranded as Shared Mobility Inc. to reflect our nationwide scope and broadened mission across the shared mobility spectrum.

Today

Nationwide Impact

Working across bikesharing, carsharing, volunteer transportation, vanpooling, and transportation demand management programs nationwide.
